Internet Of things(IOT):

The IOT (Internet Of Things) is a new technology that allows computing devices (devices that can be programmed and can connect to Internet such as smart home appliances like smart refrigerators or smart air conditioners, a smart heart monitor chip etc.), to transfer data over a network without requiring man-to-man or man-to-computer interaction

Practical Applications of IOT:

  • Health and Fitness: Iot smart gadgets like Fitbit, Jawbone, Nike and Misfit etc. that monitor your heart rate, blod pressure etc. and take action accordingly such as sending emergency messages or updating daily fitness log or contacting appointed doctor etc.
  • Home security: there are many home safety and security devices for everyone that enable video surveillence , motion , tempurature, and air quality control to help you protect your family and your home when you're not around.
  • Transport: Driverless cars can 'not only' drive on road without drivers but also can be in touch with servers all time.
  • Shopping: There are smart refrigerators nowadays that can order for grocery items as soon their quantity in fridge goes below a set level.
  • Smart Cities: The IoT technology is main enabler of making 'smart city' a reality. Smart surveillence, automated transportation, smarter energy management sstems, water distribution, urban security and environmental monitoring =, etc. are examples f Internet of Things applications for smart cities.
