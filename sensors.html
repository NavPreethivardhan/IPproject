Sensors:

Sensors are the devices which measure some physical energy (like tempurature or change in direction, etc.) and convert it into some type of signals.

We all have access to sensors around us directly or indirectly. In your school building, smoke sensors are mounted , which raise and alarm the moment some smoke is detected. Today's smartphones can sense the change in direction, your hands near the top, phone near another device and so forth. All these are sensors around us.

Sensors are used for measuring physical properties or changes in them, such as tempurature, resistance, capacitance, conduction, heat transfer,etc.

Different types of sensors that are commonly used in various applications are:
Tempurature sensor(measure change in tempurature), Proximity Sensor (measures the proximity/position of an object relative to the screen of a device), Accelermeter (measures position, motion, tilt, shock, vibration and acceleration).Pressure Sensors, (measure the pressure or change in pressure), Light Sensors(measure the change in light intensity), Ultrasonic sensors(measure ultrasonic waves>,Smoke,Gas and alcohol sensors(measure leakage of smoke, gas or alcohol),Touch Sensors(sense the position and pressure on a specific position of device), Color Sensor(senses the change in color),Humidity Sensor(senses the humidity changes),Gyroscope Sensor(senses the position, motion, tilt, shock, vbration and acceleration).
